Sava Tasev

SAVA is mainly known as a Dj who played everywhere in Bulgaria and keeps residency at important venues. For the last six years he have been actively producing and mix engineering in the genres of modern club and trap music.

Miroslav Stoynov

A professional with 8 years' experience in music production and audio engineering with a broad portfolio, ranging from producing bands and theatre scores, recording vocalists and foley, down to the nerdy technical and post-production stuff.

Alex Atanasov

Alex is an awarded double-bass player. However, he does not constrain himself to classical music. Since 2019, he participates in the songwriting process in Involved Studio and he created the Drop Down community chamber orchestra.

Alek Mladenov

One of the creators of the studio. He plays the piano and sings since 2004. Driven by psychedelic and progressive rock, with a pinch of jazz and blues. A visionary and critic of the essence produced by Involved Studio.

Gorazd Popov - Goro

Gorazd Popov is a producer and a touring DJ known in the international underground electronic music scene under the alias Goro, a project which he started in 2016. Simultaneous to this, he has had more than 10 years of experience in multi-genre production, sound design, mixing and recording.

Hristo Ivanov - Ice-P

Hailed for his experimental instrumentals, Ice-P is one of the official AMOK producers. Fusing hip-hop and R’n’B from all eras to shapes his distinguishable sound.
